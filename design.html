Graphic Design

"Kapysze" brand identity

Visual identity for the "Kapysze", traditional print studio.
By focusing on the essence of the brand and drawing inspiration from Slavic ethnic motifs and the manual print aesthetics, I was able to create a visually compelling and meaningful identity that effectively represents the studio's unique identity and values.

Behance project



Book cover and typesetting of the book
"The Knights of the Cross" by Henryk Sienkiewicz

In this project I was responsible for designing the book cover and carrying out the typesetting process.
Drawing upon my expertise in typography and layout design, I carefully crafted a visually striking cover that captured the essence of the book's themes and style, while also ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable reading experience by meticulously typesetting the text for optimal readability and visual appeal. 

Smart Coffee Maker app
UI/UX Design

A smartphone UI/UX design project for a Smart Coffee machine, aimed at incorporating various functions such as coffee library, planning, customization, and coffee-making capabilities into the app. The design process was informed by extensive research into user preferences and behaviors, as well as the latest trends in UI design. Through the use of various design elements such as color, typography, and imagery, the app was able to effectively communicate its features and functionalities to the target audience, making it both user-friendly and visually appealing.

Poster

"Young and Competent" design campaign
for the Jan Karski Foundation

The creation of an information campaign aimed at promoting an educational program for the students of 7th-grade.
In this capacity, a poster and leaflet were designed that effectively communicated the benefits of the program and motivated young people to take an active interest in it. The poster, in particular, received widespread acclaim from the target audience and was leveraged to generate interest and awareness about the program through social media platforms such as Facebook, as well as school and press materials. Vital contributions were made that played a role in increasing the program's visibility and attracting a larger number of participants.
