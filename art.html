Art for "Where the Fire burns"

A visual novel was created using Ren'Py that drew inspiration from the rich folklore of the Polissia region spanning across Ukraine, Belarus, and Poland. The plot was derived from ethnographic records from the region, ensuring an authentic representation of the culture and traditions of the area. Over 100 hand-drawn assets were created, which were then digitized and animated using After Effects, to bring the story to life. The final result was a stunning display of artistry and animation that complemented the captivating storyline. The musical score, provided by Vezha Khmar, a Ukrainian ritual ambient musician, further added to the immersive experience of the visual novel.
