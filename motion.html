Industriální pohraničí

motion design


Tools: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop

Animations of the vector icons was created for the Moravian-Silesian Tourism website called Industriální pohraničí, which depicted various tourist attractions in both the Polish and Czech regions of Silesia.
The project is aimed at promoting tourism in the region. The resulting animation effectively showcased the various attractions and highlights of the region in a visually engaging and informative manner.

"Woman in politics" animation

Tools: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop

An animation was created that delves into the issue of sexual objectification of women, even when they hold positions of high political power. Through extensive research and careful consideration, the project sheds light on the pervasive and damaging nature of this issue, highlighting the need for increased awareness and action. The animation provides a thought-provoking commentary on the objectification of women, particularly in the political sphere, where gender stereotypes and biases continue to persist.
